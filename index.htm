Memorable Password Generator

The idea behind this password generator is to come up with a password that can be visualised, i.e. you can conjure up a mental image of it, which means it should be easier to remember. And instead of writing it down somewhere, you could draw a picture of it instead.

Either enter your own name for your specific password, or leave the text box blank for a random password.
Experiment with capitalization until you find your perfect password.
The result will be a word between 9 and 18 characters long.

Obviously, don't use any password generated here for anything serious. And always change a letter or two to a number or a symbol.
